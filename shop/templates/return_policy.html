Return Policy

Our Commitment to Your Satisfaction

At ShopEase, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, we're here to help make it right.

Return Eligibility

You may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. We'll also pay the return shipping costs if the return is a result of our error (you received an incorrect or defective item, etc.).

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In its original condition and packaging
  • Unused and unworn (where applicable)
  • Accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase

Several types of goods are exempt from being returned:

  • Personal care items (makeup, skincare, etc.)
  • Food and consumables
  • Downloadable software products
  • Gift cards
  • Some health and hygiene products

Non-Returnable Items

We do not accept returns for:

  • Items that are clearly personalized or custom-made
  • Perishable goods (such as food, flowers, or plants)
  • Newspapers, magazines, or other periodicals
  • Intimate or sanitary goods
  • Hazardous materials, flammable liquids, or gases

Return Process

  1. Initiate Return: Log into your account and go to "My Orders". Select the order you wish to return and click "Request Return".
  2. Approval: Our team will review your return request and send you a return authorization within 1-2 business days.
  3. Package Items: Pack the item securely in its original packaging if possible. Include all accessories, tags, and documentation.
  4. Ship: Drop off the package at the designated carrier location. Return shipping labels will be provided in your authorization email.
  5. Refund: Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5 business days.

Refunds

We process refunds as quickly as possible. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days for the funds to appear in your account
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 7-10 business days
  • Store Credit: Immediate (if you choose this option)

If you haven't received your refund within the expected timeframe, please contact us at support@shopease.com.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received a refund yet:

  • Check your bank account again
  • Contact your credit card company - it may take some time before your refund is officially posted
  • Next contact your bank - there is often some processing time before a refund is posted
  • If you've done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at support@shopease.com

Sale Items

Only regular priced items may be refunded. Sale items cannot be refunded unless they are defective.

Exchanges

We replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item for the same item, please contact us at support@shopease.com with your order number and details of the issue.
